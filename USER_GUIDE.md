# 📖 User Guide: Malaysia Automotive Market Dashboard

Welcome to the Malaysia Automotive Market Dashboard. This guide will help you navigate the features and understand the metrics presented.

## 🧭 Navigation

- **Overview**: The high-level cockpit showing current year KPIs, adoption comparisons, and market share distribution.
- **EV Trends**: A deep dive into registration velocity. Use the **Year-to-Date** toggle to see current performance vs. last year's cycle.
- **Market Share**: Analyzes the shift from traditional Internal Combustion Engines (ICE) to Hybrid and Electric powertrains.
- **Vehicle Leaderboard**: The official ranking system for models and manufacturers. You can **Export** this data as a CSV for your own research.

## 📈 Understanding the Charts

### 📊 Adoption Comparison
- **Previous (2025)**: Represented by the subtle backdrop trend.
- **Current (2026)**: The active, highlight trend showing current market performance.
- *Tip: Look for the 'Growth Index' KPI to see the percentage increase in adoption velocity.*

### 🥧 Fuel Type Distribution
- This chart breaks down the market into **Petrol**, **Diesel**, **Green Diesel**, **Hybrid Petrol**, and **Electric**. 
- It helps you visualize how quickly the "Electric" slice of the pie is growing.

### 🗺️ Regional Analysis
- See which Malaysian states are the most "EV-Ready". 
- Note: High corporate registrations in WP Kuala Lumpur and Selangor often drive the initial adoption spikes.

## 🛠️ Data Integrity
- All data is sourced from the **Department of Statistics Malaysia (DOSM)**.
- Metrics are updated via the `sync_data.py` pipeline which aggregates raw registration records.
- "EV" refers specifically to Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) for the most accurate sustainability tracking.
